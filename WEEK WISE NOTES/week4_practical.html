Univariate and Bivariate Notes¶

Define Univariate¶

Univariate data refers to a dataset where each observation is associated with only one variable. This means it focuses on measuring or observing a single characteristic or attribute for each individual in the dataset.¶

define Bivariate¶

Bivariate means the analysis of two variables. Using bivariate analysis we can find how well the variables are correlated.¶

Bivariate analysis is of 3 types¶

1.Numerical variables¶

2.Categorical variables¶

3.Numerical & Categorical variable¶
